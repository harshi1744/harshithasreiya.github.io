About Me

Hi, I’m Harshitha Sreiya Palaparthi, a Business Analytics graduate student pursuing my Master’s at the University of North Texas. I specialize in AI applications, data visualization, and business development, with strong technical skills in Python, SQL, Tableau, and cloud platforms including Google Cloud.

My experience spans analyzing complex datasets, building predictive models, and enabling data-driven decision-making across various business functions. I’ve led impactful projects like an AI-powered resume screening assistant that reduced hiring time, a fraud detection solution to safeguard financial integrity, and interactive dashboards that visualize malnutrition trends across regions.

Alongside my studies, I gained real-world experience as a Business Development Intern at NSIC and a Learning & Development Associate at Voice 4 Girls, where I enhanced program evaluation and engagement strategies through data analysis.

I’m currently open to internship opportunities and actively seeking full-time roles starting December 2025 across the U.S., where I can leverage my analytical expertise, technical skills, and collaborative mindset to tackle real-world business challenges.
