About Me

Hi, I’m Harshitha Sreiya Palaparthi, a Business Analytics graduate student pursuing my Master’s at the University of North Texas. I specialize in AI applications, data visualization, and business development, with strong technical skills in Python, SQL, Tableau, and cloud platforms including Google Cloud.

My experience spans analyzing complex datasets, building predictive models, and enabling data-driven decision-making across various business functions. Alongside my studies, I gained real-world experience as a Business Development Intern at NSIC and a Learning & Development Associate at Voice 4 Girls, where I enhanced program evaluation and engagement strategies through data analysis.

I’m currently open to internship opportunities and actively seeking full-time roles starting December 2025 across the U.S.
